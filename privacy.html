Privacy Policy

At Udaan Coaching, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information you provide to us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you register, inquire, or use our platform:

  • Personal Details: Name, Date of Birth, Gender, and Academic background.
  • Contact Information: Mobile number, Email address, and Residential address.
  • Usage Data: Test scores, course progress, app/website usage metrics, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is securely used for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your student profile and course access.
  • To communicate upcoming batch details, test schedules, and important announcements.
  • To analyze performance and provide personalized mentoring and feedback.
  • To process fee payments and issue receipts securely.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement stringent security measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect your personal and payment data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. Your data may only be shared with trusted service providers (like payment gateways or sms services) strictly for operational purposes under confidentiality agreements.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, track site usage, and remember your login session. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request a review, update, or deletion of your personal information stored with us by contacting our support team at info@udaan.com.
